Mancala Rules

1. Each player has six pits filled with four stones each.

2. Players take turns picking up all stones from one of their pits and distributing them counterclockwise.

3. If the last stone lands in your store, you get another turn.

4. If the last stone lands in an empty pit on your side, you capture that stone and any in the opposite pit.

5. The game ends when one side is empty, and the player with the most stones in their store wins.
