Game project

Super Metal Detector

An indie exploration and rhythm game about beach bums, buried mysteries, and building something weird and memorable with a longtime friend.

About

One of my oldest friends and I have wanted to make a game together for years. He's a graphic designer and a seriously talented artist, and I'm the one trying to turn the ideas into a playable world. We live far apart now, but we still carve out time to keep building this project together.

What it is

Super Metal Detector is an exploration and rhythm game where you play as a beach wanderer with a love for finding strange metallic treasures. Along the way you meet other beach locals, help them out, and venture into hidden caverns buried under the shoreline.

Current progress

We're still early, so this is more proof-of-direction than polished vertical slice. Even so, the tone, art direction, and core idea are all starting to take shape, and that's enough momentum to keep going.
