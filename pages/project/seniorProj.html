Food Waste Reducer AI

A proof-of-concept project exploring how decentralized decision systems can reduce food waste while respecting realistic household habits.

Perishable food reduction

The goal of this project is to develop backend logic for an application that minimizes perishable food waste and cost while still allowing users to maintain realistic diet habits. Many food management tools assume that purchases are always used exactly as planned, but real life rarely works that neatly.

Cooperative reward sharing in decentralized MDPs

Markov Decision Processes are computationally expensive, especially as a pantry grows in size. In this implementation, each perishable item is represented by its own MDP. At reward time, the system aggregates outcomes across all item-level models and redistributes a modified reward based on the shared result.

Where it stands

This project is currently a proof of concept for the reward-sharing approach. I'm interested in exploring additional implementations and seeing how the idea performs in more realistic planning environments.
